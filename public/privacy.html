Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use AuthPenguin, we may collect the following information:

  • OAuth Provider Data: Username, email, profile picture, and other public profile information from your chosen OAuth provider
  • Authentication Tokens: Temporary tokens used to maintain your authentication session
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our service
  • Scratch Verification: For Scratch authentication, we check public comments on your profile

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide OAuth authentication services
  • To verify your identity with third-party providers
  • To maintain your authentication session
  • To improve our service and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Authentication tokens are stored securely using Redis
  • Tokens expire automatically after 24 hours
  • We use secure HTTPS connections for all communications
  • We do not store sensitive OAuth credentials

4. Third-Party Services

AuthPenguin integrates with the following third-party OAuth providers:

  • Discord: Subject to Discord's Privacy Policy
  • Google: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • GitHub: Subject to GitHub's Privacy Policy
  • Scratch: We access public profile information and comments

Please review the privacy policies of these services for information about their data practices.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for the following periods:

  • Authentication tokens: 24 hours or until logout
  • Scratch verification codes: 10 minutes
  • Profile information: Only during active authentication sessions

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • The right to access your data
  • The right to delete your data (by logging out)
  • The right to revoke OAuth permissions through the respective providers
  • The right to contact us with privacy concerns

7. Children's Privacy

AuthPenguin does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository or other available channels.
